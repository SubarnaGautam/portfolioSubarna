ABOUT SUBORN

Culturally motivated and passionate chef Subarna Gautam was born in Nepal and began his culinary journey in Sydney, working across a variety of establishments, including pubs, cafés, bars, and fine dining restaurants. His dedication and expertise have since led him to work in Canberra Hotels, where he continues to bring his diverse culinary skills to life. Subarna’s career is a testament to his versatility and commitment to excellence in the culinary arts.
